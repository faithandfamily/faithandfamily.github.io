Frequently
Asked
Questions:

Do you have a waitlist?

We do have an on-going waitlist. We fill spots as often as we have availability and there is a $40 non-refundable fee to secure your spot on the waitlist.

What ages do you accept?

We offer care for children 6 weeks of age all the way to Pre-K (4 turning 5 going to kindergarten). We do not offer after school care.

Do you offer part-time care?

Unfortunately due to being a smaller center we are not able to offer part time care for children.

What are your classroom ratios?

We follow the licensing minimum standards:
Age Ratio
0-11 months 1 teacher : 4 children
12-18 months 1 teacher : 5 children
18-23 months 1 teacher : 9 children
2 year olds 1 teacher : 11 children
3 year olds 1 teacher : 15 children
4 year olds 1 teacher : 18 children

What curriculum do you use?

We use a curriculum called Frog Street, it is a research based curriculum that serves children birth through age five. The curriculum supports key learning domains and is aligned with national learning standards.

How many staff members are CPR/First Aid Certified?

Minimum Standards requires all staff members to be certified to be left alone in the classroom! We also require all new hires to get this within their first 90 days of being hired.

What app do you use to communicate with parents?

We use Procare, it is an app offered through Apple and Google App stores. The teachers use this to communicate daily activities such as: feedings, diaper changes.
